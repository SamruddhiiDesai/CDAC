About Us

"Sandbox" refers to a creative and experimental space where students can freely display their academic projects, receive peer feedback, and make improvements based on insights from others. The "sandbox" environment encourages exploration, collaboration, and refinement, enabling students to experiment with ideas, showcase their work, and iterate without the pressure of formal assessments.
