The robot is equipped with advanced voice recognition technology that can understand and interpret commands in multiple languages, making it accessible to a global audience. Users can communicate with the robot in their preferred language.

Aim Of the Project
The aim of the Farmbook project is to create an accessible web platform for farmers and agricultural stakeholders that delivers comprehensive, reliable information on crop diseases, including identification, prevention, and treatment methods. By leveraging digital technology, Farmbook seeks to empower users with actionable insights and improve crop health management.
Objectives
1.Disease Information Repository
To provide a detailed and categorized repository of over 100 crop diseases, including causes, symptoms, and images for easy identification.
2. Preventive and Corrective Measures
To offer practical solutions and preventive measures for each disease, aiding farmers in maintaining healthy crops and reducing losses.
3.User-Friendly Interface
To design an intuitive and responsive web interface that makes it easy for users to find and access relevant disease information across multiple devices.
4.Feedback and Interaction Mechanism
To enable users to submit feedback and interact with the platform, allowing continuous improvement of the information provided and catering to the evolving needs of the farming community.
